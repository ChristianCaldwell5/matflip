Mat Flip

Challenge your memory and/or math skills!

How to Play:

  1. Select a game mode
  2. Select a difficulty level
  3. Select Start Game when ready!

Game Modes:

  • Pairs: select cards to find matching pairs before time runs out!

    • Easy: 5 pairs; 25 seconds
    • Medium: 6 pairs; 40 seconds
    • Hard: 8 pairs; 60 seconds
    • Mastery: 10 pairs; 90 seconds
  • Solution: memorize displayed answers shown for a brief period before they flip (5 seconds). Once they flip over, you must solve a math problem, recall the location of the correct answer, and select it before time runs out. Get as many solutions as you can before you run out of allowed fails!
    • Easy: 4 fails allowed; 20 seconds to solve problems; add & sub; no negative digits
    • Medium: 4 fails allowed; 20 seconds to solve problems; add & sub; negative digits enabled
    • Hard: 3 fails allowed; 15 seconds to solve problems; add, sub, mult, div; negative digits enabled
    • Mastery: 2 fails allowed; 10 seconds to solve problems; add, sub, mult, div; negative digits enabled
