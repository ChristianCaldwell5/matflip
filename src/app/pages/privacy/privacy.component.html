Privacy Policy

Last updated: 08.01.2025

Matflip is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

a. Google Account Info (Signed-in users only)

If you choose to create an account or sign in using Google, we collect:

  • Your Google Account Email address
  • Name associated with your Google Account
  • Profile picture associated with your Google Account

This information is only used to create, personalize, and manage your Matflip account. If you do not sign in, Matflip does not collect this data.

b. Analytics Data (All visitors)

Matflip uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. Google Analytics collects non-identifying information such as:

  • IP address (processed by Google; not stored by us)
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Click events and site interactions
  • Country/city/state

Matflip does not use analytics for advertising.

c. Cookies

Matflip uses cookies for:

  • Session management (Signed-in users only)
  • Site functionality
  • Analytics

You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not work.

2. How We Use Your Information

Matflip uses the information we collect only to:

  • Create and maintain your Matflip account
  • Provide access to site features
  • Conduct anonymous analytics

Matflip does not sell or share your personal information with third parties for advertising, marketing, or any unrelated purpose.

3. Third-Party Services

Matflip uses:

  • Google Sign-In
    • Used for secure authentication and account management
    • Google's Privacy Policy applies to their handling of your information
  • Google Analytics
    • Used for site performance and usage metrics

4. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information (1a) we have about you
  • Request deletion of your Matflip account and associated data
